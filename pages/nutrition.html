Nutritional Information

We know it’s perfectly possible to include pizza in a balanced diet, and at Domino's we're committed to providing transparent nutritional information, enabling our customers to make informed decisions. The ingredients in all our products can be found here.

To ensure this transparency, we provide calorie information on our website, on our menus and on our app. We publish detailed nutritional information for more than 1,000 combinations of regular pizzas and those made with our Delight Cheese

We’re proud that we do not to use artificial colours or flavourings in any of our pizzas or sides, and removed processed trans-fats several years ago.
